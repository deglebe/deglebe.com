about

hello! i'm thomas (deglebe) bruce. i am a 20 year old computer scientist/engineer/software developer (formal training is in software engineering) based in upstate new york.

my primary computing interests are operating systems, low power (perma)computing, and language design, and as such my particular domain of expertise lies in low-level development. i am also fairly knowledgeable about the history of cs and open-source, as well as machine learning applications in data science.

my non-cs computer interests lie in occasional gaming, collecting pdfs, reading blogs, listening to music, and making charts for various things

off the computer, i am an avid endurance runner and hiker (nordic skiing in the winter). i enjoy reading any literature i can get my hands on, tinkering with electronics, writing, studying languages, and cooking.

about this site
